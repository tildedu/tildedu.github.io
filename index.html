Vergil's Product & Engineering Blog

Vergil is Columbia Universities new Student Information System (SIS). Vergil was developed internally by a dedicated product team consisting of 60 software engineers, application architects, cloud engineers, product managers, technical analysts, as well multiple consulting partners and accelerators. Vergil is cloud native and the application is comprised of multiple microservices that are indistinguishable to the end user

Publius Vergilius Maro

Publius Vergilius Maro was an ancient Roman poet of the Augustan period. He composed three of the most famous poems in Latin literature: the Eclogues (or Bucolics), the Georgics, and the epic Aeneid. A number of minor poems, collected in the Appendix Vergiliana, were attributed to him in ancient times, but modern scholars consider his authorship of these poems as dubious.
